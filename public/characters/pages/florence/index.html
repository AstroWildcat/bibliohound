Shy, tender, and quiet: underneath Florence Wildberry's demure nature is a hardened, fiery survivalist at heart. She finds it hard to trust people, and dislikes how much society breaks those who struggle to survive by overworking them. She strongly dislikes those who are wasteful and are inconsiderate of others. She lives deep in the woods in a cottage. She enjoys arts and crafts, cooking, gardening, reading, and a variety of other hobbies.

Trivia

  • She is loosely inspired by Edwardian fashion and the cottagecore aesthetic.
