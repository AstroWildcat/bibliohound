Active Social Media

While I have a lot of social media accounts, most of them are inactive thanks to my fickle attention span with most websites. The ones linked below are the accounts I tend to prioritize.

Goodreads

A place for me to update my reading list. I personally prefer Storygraph, but alas, Goodreads is what everyone uses to keep track of their reading progress. So as someone who wants to read, I have no real choice in the matter.

Letterboxd

This website has single-handedly given me motivation to work on watching movies again. I'm serious. It challenges me to have something to say about a movie once I watch, and to analyze it on a five star scale.

Storygraph

My preferred place to update my reading progress. That being said, I am still a really slow reader, and it's kind of embarassing at this point. I also like the UI better, it's less cluttered.

Pinterest

I never expected myself to be a fan of organizing images but here we are. Anyways, I'm usually on here to make aesthetic boards for my characcters and stories.

Tumblr (Personal Blog)

I've been using Tumblr since 2015, and that's not going to change anytime soon. If you want to reach out to me privately, your best bet is to message me here.

Tumblr (OC Blog)

I tend to blog about my characters, as I am rather fixated on them. That being said, the most I do on there is tag them in posts I think fit them. Sometimes I post art and musings about them as well.

Writing Portfolio

This is where I keep all of my actual writing and prose. There's not much on it so far, because I haven't made a lot of short stories I feel are actually worth publishing.
