Desires of His Heart

Delight yourself also in the Lord, And He shall give you the desires of your heart. (New King James Version, Psalm 37:4)

After the unexpected death of her husband in 1952, Judith Mitchell decides to get involved with her local church in the small, Midwestern town of Edenheim. However, she ends up becoming acquainted with the alluring and hauntingly familiar Lillian, the parish's associate. The two women end up in a complicated, secret relationship, one that only serves to grow in intensity as time passes. With Judith's increasing hunger pains and delusions, there is only one question: will she give into her instincts?

Characters

Judith

She is a devoted Catholic with an unusual level of fixation on saints. As her secret relatinonship with the priest's sister continues, the delusions of her being the reincarnation of St. Agatha grows. So does the yearning to consume her flesh.

Lillian

Otherwise known as Lily, she is the sister and parish associate of the local priest. She is cold, humble, subdued with her emotions. Despite her passionate faith for God and her service to the community, she finds it more difficult to grapple her inner feelings.

Aaron

Aaron is the lead priest for the most well-known Catholic church in Edenheim, which has been run by his family for decades. When he is not working on perfecting his faith, he is trying to climb the social ladder in his small town.

Francesca

She is a now deceased student and former roommate of Judith at Our Lady St. Agnes of Rome Academy. She was infamous among the student body and faculty for her flippant attitude and mysterious demeanor.

Meta Analysis

To be honest, I don't have a lot to say about this one. It's partially inspired by my sudden and impending obsession with toxic yuri and Catholicism. I think both of those things are pretty interesting. It also serves as a replacement for an older story, Erotomanie, which I could never figure out how to plot properly. This story, on the other hand, has at least a few route endings for the visual novel release. So hopefully this story ends up as some sort of final product.
